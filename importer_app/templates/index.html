Welcome to the Circuit Invoice Linker

Use the tabs in the navigation bar to import and reconcile your data.

Recommended Workflow:

  1. Customers > Zeus Imports: Import SCI and Circuit data from your CSVs.
  2. Suppliers > Invoice Import: Import supplier invoice CSVs using your defined mappings.
  3. Suppliers > Standard/Bulk Linker: Reconcile invoice items to their corresponding CMDB circuits.
  4. Customers > Zeus Exports: Review, audit, and perform bulk updates on your reconciled data.
